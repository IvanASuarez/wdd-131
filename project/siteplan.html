Belleza Andina

Site Purpose

Site Purpose: The purpose of this website is to provide women with easy access to professional beauty services, offering detailed information about each treatment, its benefits, and available pricing options. The site will allow customers to conveniently book appointments and make online payments, ensuring they can look and feel their best for everyday life or special occasions.

objectives

  • Offer a variety of professional beauty services for hands, feet, eyes, and body, available both at the salon and through home visits.
  • Provide clear and engaging information about each service, including a description of the procedure, benefits, duration, and prices.
  • Enable an easy-to-use online booking system for customers to schedule appointments at their convenience.
  • Integrate a secure online payment system to allow customers to pay for selected services in advance.
  • Enhance the customer experience with visual content, promotions, and personalized recommendations based on their interests.
